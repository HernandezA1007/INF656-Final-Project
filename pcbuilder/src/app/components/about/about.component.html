About PC Builder

PC Builder is a web application that allows users to build their own PC. Users can select from a list of components and add them to their build. The application will then calculate the total cost of the build and display it to the user. Users can also save their build and view it later. You don't need to build a computer to want to learn and understand how it works. Whether it's about parts or looking for pre-builds or looking for resources or services such as troubleshooting.

Purpose

PC Builder is committed to demystifying the process of building and upgrading computers. We provide tools, resources, and guides to empower you to make informed decisions, whether you're assembling your first PC or selecting pre-built components.
